About Me

"Hello! My name is Ankith Maremanda, and I am currently associated with Johns Hopkins University. I started Human Anatomy for Youth as a freshman in high school because I wanted to convey my own passion for the field of human biology to others, and to level the "academic playing field" in a sense so everybody can get an equal opportunity to learn this science which is so important to how we function every day."
